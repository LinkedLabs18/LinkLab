Introduction

Welcome to LinkLab! We value your privacy and want to be transparent about the way we use cookies and other tools on our website. This Privacy Policy explains how cookies are used and how we handle your data while you explore our content and affiliate recommendations.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you directly. We do not require you to provide any personal data to access our website, browse content, or explore affiliate links.

Cookies

Some third-party services we use may collect data via cookies to improve your experience on our site. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help track activity, personalize your experience, and provide affiliate tracking features. These services may include:

  • Affiliate links: When you click on our affiliate links (e.g., Amazon), cookies may be used to track your activity and ensure we receive credit for purchases made through our links.
  • Google Analytics: When we use Google Analytics to monitor and analyze how visitors use our website. This helps us improve our content and user experience. Google Analytics may use cookies to track information about your visit, such as the pages you view and how long you spend on the site.
  • Advertising: When we use third-party advertising services that use cookies to show relevant ads to you based on your interests and browsing behavior.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect some features of the website.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Track affiliate purchases and ensure proper commission credit.
  • Analyze site usage through Google Analytics.
  • Improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences or showing relevant ads.

Your Rights

As we do not collect personal information, you do not have to take action to access, modify, or delete data. However, you can control how cookies are used by adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies if you wish.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party websites (including affiliate links). These external sites may use their own cookies to track user activity and gather data. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
